Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple websites can be completed in 2-4 weeks, while complex applications with AI components may take 2-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our discovery phase.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed project fees, hourly rates, and monthly retainers for ongoing services. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we work with clients globally. While we're based in Lagos, Nigeria, we have successfully delivered projects for clients in the US, UK, Europe, and other African countries. We use modern collaboration tools to ensure seamless communication regardless of location.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We specialize in modern web technologies including React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, TensorFlow, and cloud platforms like AWS and Google Cloud. We're constantly evaluating and adopting new technologies to deliver the best solutions for our clients.

How do you handle project communication?

We believe communication is key to successful projects. We use a combination of tools including Slack for daily communication, Zoom for video meetings, and project management platforms like Jira or Trello for tracking progress. We provide regular updates and involve clients throughout the development process.
