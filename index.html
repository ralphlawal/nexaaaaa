FAQ

Questions answered.

Everything you need to know before starting a project. Don’t see yours? Just ask us directly.

Ask Us Directly
How long does a typical project take?
Landing pages take 1–2 weeks. Full web applications take 4–10 weeks. Complex platforms take 3–6 months. We give you a realistic timeline at the discovery stage before any work or payment begins.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes. We are fully remote-capable and work with clients in Ireland, Nigeria, the UK and internationally. We communicate via email, WhatsApp and video calls across time zones.
What do I need to get started?
A clear idea of what you want to build. We handle everything from scoping through to launch. If you have brand assets or an existing brief, that helps — but they are not required to begin a conversation.
Do you offer post-launch support?
Yes. Every engagement includes 30–60 days of post-launch support as standard. Enterprise clients can access monthly retainer agreements for ongoing development and priority issue resolution.
Can you work within a fixed budget?
Yes — tell us your budget upfront and we will scope a project that delivers maximum value within it. We would rather be transparent about constraints than overpromise and underdeliver.
What makes NexaCore Labs different?
We are a focused studio, not a volume agency. Every project gets genuine strategic thinking, a dual-market perspective you will not find elsewhere, and the engineering standards of a team that builds and maintains real products used by real people.
