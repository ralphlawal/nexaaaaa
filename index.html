Everything you need to know before starting a project with us. Don't see your question? Just ask.

Ask Us Directly
How long does a typical project take?
It depends on scope, but as a guide: landing pages take 1–2 weeks, full web applications take 4–10 weeks, and complex platforms like Examora take 3–6 months. We'll give you a realistic timeline during our discovery call before any work begins.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely. We're fully remote-capable and have worked with clients in Ireland, Nigeria, the UK, and beyond. We communicate clearly via email, WhatsApp, and video calls — timezone differences are manageable.
What do you need from me to get started?
Just a clear idea of what you want to build. We'll handle the rest — from scoping to design to launch. If you have a brief, brand assets, or existing materials, even better. We'll guide you through our discovery process from day one.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Yes. Every project comes with 30–60 days of post-launch support depending on your plan. For enterprise clients, we offer monthly retainer agreements that cover updates, new features, and priority bug fixes.
Can you work within a fixed budget?
Yes. Tell us your budget upfront and we'll design a scope that delivers maximum value within it. We'd rather be transparent about what's achievable than overpromise and underdeliver.
What makes NexaCore Labs different?
We're not an agency with 50 clients on a conveyor belt. We're a focused studio that treats every project like our own product — with strategic thinking, AI-first architecture, and a dual-market perspective that most agencies simply don't have.
