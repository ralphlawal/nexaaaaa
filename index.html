Ask Us Directly
How long does a typical project take?
Landing pages take 1–2 weeks, full web apps take 4–10 weeks, complex platforms take 3–6 months. We give a realistic timeline at our discovery call before any work begins.
Do you work with international clients?
Absolutely. We're fully remote-capable and work with clients in Ireland, Nigeria, the UK and beyond via email, WhatsApp and video calls.
What do I need to get started?
Just a clear idea of what you want to build. We handle everything from scoping to design to launch. Brand assets are a bonus if you have them.
Do you offer post-launch support?
Yes. Every project includes 30–60 days of post-launch support. Enterprise clients can access monthly retainer agreements for ongoing updates and priority fixes.
Can you work within a fixed budget?
Yes — tell us your budget upfront and we'll design a scope that delivers maximum value within it. We'd rather be transparent than overpromise.
What makes NexaCore Labs different?
We're a focused studio — not a conveyor-belt agency. Every project gets strategic thinking, AI-first architecture and a dual-market perspective most agencies simply don't have.
